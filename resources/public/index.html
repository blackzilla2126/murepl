MUREPL is a clojure repl that runs against a restricted namespace of MUD commands.

Command Explanation Example
(new-player "name" "password" "description") Create a new player and logs you in. Passwords are passed in cleartext (new-player "GraceHopper" "bugsquasher" "Important computer scientist.")
(connect "name" "password") Log in as a player you have already created. (connect "GraceHopper" "bugsquasher")
(look) Look around at the are you are in. (look)
(say "message") Say something out loud. Others in the same room as you will hear. (say "Greetings and salutations")
(go :direction) Leave the area via the exit in the given direction. (go :north)
(examine "player") Look at another player. Can examine "self" to examine yourself. (examine "RichHickey")
(create-room "name" "desc" {:direction "room name"}) Add a new room to the world. The exit map must point to an existing room. (create-room "alleyway" "A dark and trash-littered alley." {:south "Lobby"})
